🦷 Dental Management

Dental Hospital is a comprehensive Dental Clinic Management system built for Odoo. It helps you manage dental appointments, patient records, treatments, prescriptions, and payments efficiently, all within Odoo. Designed for dental hospitals and clinics, it ensures smooth coordination between receptionists, dentists, and administrators.

🚀 Why Use Dental Hospital?

  • Seamless handling of patient appointments with automatic patient info filling.
  • Role-based access for Receptionists, Dentists, and Managers.
  • Track treatments, prescriptions, and payments all in one place.
  • Detailed treatment and payment logs for every patient.

✅ Key Features

  • 📅 Appointment Management: Create, confirm, and track dental appointments with assigned dentists and urgency levels.
  • 💊 Prescription Management: Link treatments with prescriptions and medicines.
  • 💳 Payment Logging: Maintain detailed logs for partial and full payments, track balance dues.
  • 🦷 Tooth Mapping: Track affected teeth via a visual Many2many tooth selection model.
  • 📈 Dashboard Ready: Can be extended with OWL-based dashboards for real-time reporting.

📦 Feature Updates

👤 User Access Role

  • Receptionist: Can create patients and appointments.
  • Dentist: Can only view and manage their appointments and treatments.
  • Manager: Full access to all features.
  • Patient: Can log in via portal to view appointments, treatments, and payments.

🌐 Patient Portal Access

  • Patients can securely log in to the portal.
  • View appointment history, treatment details, and invoices.
  • Download prescription PDFs and view balance dues.

📆 Appointment Booking in the Portal

  • Patients can book appointments online via the website.
  • Select preferred date, time slot, and available dentist.
  • Receive confirmation and reminders via email or WhatsApp (if integrated).

📌 Use Case Scenario

  • A receptionist books an appointment and selects an available dentist.
  • The dentist treats the patient and records the prescription and treatment details.
  • Payment is logged against the treatment, and a balance is tracked if any.
  • The manager reviews the performance and financial records from the backend.
